EVENT LOG COMPARISON
================================================================================

Cold (1st settle - 9 logs):

1. Transfer (APOW):           user -> pool (principal)
2. Transfer (APOW):           interest distribution
3. Transfer (bAPOW):          debt burn
4. Sync (bAPOW):              index update
5. Transfer (APOW):           fee movement
6. Transfer (sAPOW):          supply update
7. LargeHolderRemoved:        holder tracking
8. Transfer (sAPOW):          EXTRA: interest to suppliers *
9. Settle (Pool):             main event

Warm (8th settle - 8 logs):

1. Transfer (APOW):           user -> pool (principal)
2. Transfer (APOW):           interest distribution
3. Transfer (bAPOW):          debt burn
4. Sync (bAPOW):              index update
5. Transfer (APOW):           fee movement
6. Transfer (sAPOW):          supply update
7. LargeHolderRemoved:        holder tracking
8. Settle (Pool):             main event

Key Difference: The 8th log (extra SupplyPosition Transfer) is missing in
warm/dust settlements. This transfer distributes accrued interest to suppliers
and is only triggered when settling amounts above a threshold.

================================================================================
WHY SETTLE COSTS LESS THAN BORROW
================================================================================

Operation    | Cold Gas  | Warm Gas  | Difference
-------------|-----------|-----------|------------
Borrow       | 278,858   | 249,199   | 29,659
Settle       | 256,451   | 233,603   | 22,848

Settle is cheaper than borrow because:
1. No health check required - Settling improves position, no oracle calls
2. Simpler token flow - Burn debt tokens vs mint new tokens
3. No collateralization validation - Just reduce existing debt

================================================================================
DUST SETTLEMENT PATTERN
================================================================================

The user settled in 8 transactions to clear all debt:

  1st: 333.00 APOW      (bulk of debt)
  2nd:   0.3327 APOW    (interest accrued)
  3rd:   0.000333 APOW  (smaller interest)
  4th-8th: wei amounts  (dust cleanup)

This pattern occurs because:
1. Interest accrues between transactions
2. Rounding in index calculations leaves dust
3. Multiple small settlements needed to reach exact zero
4. Final 2 wei settlement completes the payoff
