================================================================================
WHY ALL STORAGE IS WARM
================================================================================

Despite being the user's first settle, all borrow-position storage for cold2
is already warm from the prior borrow() call in setUp:

  - _balances[cold2]    was written during borrow → WARM
  - _principalOf[cold2]  was written during borrow → WARM
  - _userIndex[cold2]    was written during borrow → WARM
  - _largeHolders       was written during borrow → WARM

Settle only burns borrow position tokens — it does NOT create new user-specific
storage slots. All slots being modified were already initialized by borrow.

This is why settle cold and settle warm have IDENTICAL gas costs.

================================================================================
KEY OBSERVATIONS
================================================================================

1. Settle is the cheapest pool operation (~170k gas)
2. No health check required (settling reduces debt, improves health)
3. No rate limiting or PoW limiting (only nonReentrant)
4. All user-specific storage is already warm from the prior borrow
5. Gas cost is IDENTICAL to warm settle (~170,266, within 3 gas)
6. Token transfers + vault deposit account for ~57% of total gas
7. BorrowPosition.burn() is the main computation at ~26%
