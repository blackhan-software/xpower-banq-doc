================================================================================
WHY ALL STORAGE IS WARM
================================================================================

Despite being the user's first redeem, all supply-position storage for cold2
is already warm from the prior supply() call in setUp:

  - _balances[cold2]    was written during supply → WARM
  - _principalOf[cold2]  was written during supply → WARM
  - _userIndex[cold2]    was written during supply → WARM

Redeem only burns supply position tokens — it does NOT create new user-specific
storage slots. All slots being modified were already initialized by supply.

================================================================================
KEY OBSERVATIONS
================================================================================

1. This is a "cold" redeem but all user storage is actually WARM
2. Supply-position storage was initialized during the prior supply() call
3. _checkHealth() dominates gas cost at ~38% (oracle queries)
4. Vault.redeem() is the second largest consumer at ~27%
5. Gas cost is virtually identical to warm redeem (314,558)
6. No rate limiting or PoW limiting (only nonReentrant)
7. Redeem requires health check (reduces collateral, may affect solvency)
